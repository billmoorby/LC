# Strategy

* Optimized Approach:
  - Use 2 pointers(left, right) to iterate over the list since the array is sorted in increasing order.

  - Sum both the values at the left and right pointer.

  - Check if the sum equals the target, if it does return the two indices and increase them both by 1(1-indexed).

  - Else, if the sum is less than the target, move the left pointer to increase the sum.

  - Else if the sum is greater than the target, move the right pointer to decrease the sum.
